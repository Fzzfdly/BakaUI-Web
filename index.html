v3.2.0 — The Progression Update

📅 January 30, 2026 🎮 Minecraft 1.21.132
This massive update redefines the server's economy and progression. We are introducing a "Play-to-Rank" questing ecosystem, a fully scripted Admin Event system, a high-stakes Casino, and a revamped Bounty system to enforce justice in the wilds.

🔰 The Progression Overhaul

Progression
  • Play-to-Rank: Ranks are no longer bought with money. You must earn Quest XP to climb the 12-tier ladder (Crew ➔ Exarch).
  • Dynamic Daily Quests: Every day at 12:00 AM (MYT), receive 5 unique quests across Mining, Slayer, Farming, Lumberjack, and Misc categories.
  • Progressive Difficulty: As you rank up, quests automatically evolve (e.g., "Mine Coal" becomes "Mine Diamonds") and offer significantly higher rewards.
  • Milestone Feedback: Added a smart notification system that alerts you at 25%, 50%, and 75% progress.

⚔️ Bounty System 2.0

Combat / Economy
  • The "Outlaw" Mechanic: Killing an innocent player (someone with $0 bounty) now immediately marks you as an Outlaw.
  • Automatic Fine: You will be fined 20% of your bank (or a minimum of $5,000).
  • Combat Debuffs: You will be cursed with Weakness and Mining Fatigue for 30 seconds, making escape difficult.
  • Public Alert: Your crime and coordinates are broadcast to the entire server.
  • Live Tracking: The Bounty Leaderboard now displays the Last Known Location (Dimension & Coordinates) of wanted players.
  • Manual Contracts: Players can pay to place bounties on their enemies via the new Bounty Menu.
  • Exploit Patches: Fixed critical economy exploits; players can no longer set bounties they cannot afford.

🌟 Special Quests

Admin Tools
  • Global Events: Admins can now assign Server-Wide Special Quests (e.g., "Slay the Wither") that appear at the top of your Quest Menu.
  • Admin Tools: Added a dedicated Quest Creator UI (accessed via ?adminquests) allowing staff to create custom challenges on the fly.

🎰 Casino 2.0

Minigames
  • Slot Machine: Test your luck with the new 3-reel Slot Machine! Match 3 symbols for a Jackpot (10x payout).
  • Gacha Lucky Boxes: Pay to open a mystery box with rewards scaled by rarity: Common to Legendary.
  • Coinflip: Retained and optimized the classic "Double or Nothing" coinflip system.

🛠️ Bug Fixes & Optimizations

Performance
  • Rank System: Completely removed legacy "Buy Rank" code to optimize performance.
  • Scoreboard: Minor appearance tweaks and backend performance improvements.
  • Land System: Added caching to reduce CPU usage during land checks.
  • Daily Rewards: Fixed an issue where the daily reward streak would sometimes reset incorrectly.
  • Streaks: Patched bugs related to playtime and login streaks.
